# Gap trio + same-direction pairs (user list, 2026-06-13). Daily gaps; crypto 4h has weak gaps (noted).
GAP-A Gap and go continuation: when a bar opens beyond the prior bar's close by at least 0.75 ATR AND closes in the top quarter of its range (gap up) the opening imbalance reflects fresh initiative that continues; buy the close of the gap bar, trail at 1.5 ATR, time-exit after 5 bars, mirror for gap-downs. Distinct from the dead gap-window-as-support: this trades continuation at the gap bar's own close, not a later pullback.
GAP-B Gap up mean reversion: when a bar gaps up at least 1.5 ATR but closes in the LOWER half of its range, the gap is an exhausted overreaction that fades; short the close, cover at the prior close (gap fill) or after 3 bars, ATR stop above the high; mirror gap-downs that close strong. The close-location filter is what separates this from the already-dead naive gap fade.
GAP-C Exhaustion gap reversal: a gap in the direction of an already-extended move (price more than 2 ATR from its 20-bar mean) on elevated range marks blow-off exhaustion; fade it - short an up-gap after an extended rally, cover at the 20-bar mean, ATR stop beyond the gap high. The prior-trend-extension context distinguishes exhaustion gaps from continuation gaps.
PAIR-A Same-direction pair confirmation: trade an instrument only when a correlated partner confirms the same direction - enter long when BOTH this instrument AND its highest-correlation peer in the basket are above their 50-bar averages and this instrument just reclaimed its average, exit when either falls below; the cross-confirmation filters false individual signals. Tests whether requiring a correlated second opinion improves a simple trend reclaim.
