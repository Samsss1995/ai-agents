# Derived from the Central Patterns / Central Candlesticks catalogs (2026-06-12).
# Selected for mechanics NOT yet falsified in the store: gaps, conviction bars,
# 3-bar continuation, multi-touch levels, geometric convergence. Re-skins of dead
# families (engulfing/hammer/star reversals, NR breakouts) deliberately excluded.
CHART-1 Gap window as support and resistance (fenetre + tasuki): when a bar opens beyond the prior bar's extreme leaving an unfilled gap of at least half an ATR, the gap zone marks forced repositioning and unfilled resting orders; if the gap survives 3 bars unfilled, buy the first pullback into the top edge of an up-gap with a stop below the gap's lower edge (mirror for down-gaps), targeting the post-gap extreme. The losers are those who faded the gap expecting an immediate fill.
CHART-2 Three soldiers continuation (trois soldats / trois corbeaux): three consecutive same-direction closes where each bar's body exceeds the 20-bar median body, each open sits within the prior body, and each close lands in the outer quarter of its bar's range, signal persistent initiative flow rather than exhaustion; enter in the pattern direction at the third close with a stop below the pattern's first open, exit on the first opposite conviction bar or a 2.5-ATR trail. Distinct from the dead 4-down-day fade: this trades WITH the sequence and demands body quality.
CHART-3 Multi-touch level anticipation (proximite support/resistance): a horizontal level touched at least 3 times within a lookback window without breaking concentrates resting limit orders; buy a limit at the third-or-later approach to the support level with a tight stop one ATR beyond it and a target at the opposite side of the range (mirror at multi-touch resistance); abandon the level permanently once it breaks. The edge claim is positional: entering AT the level with tight invalidation buys where the defenders are, unlike the dead post-breakout role-reversal entry.
CHART-4 Triangle convergence breakout (franchissement de triangle): when the rolling window of highs is falling while the window of lows is rising (geometrically converging extremes, not just low volatility), coiled positioning resolves directionally; enter on the first close beyond the converging boundary in the breakout direction, stop at the triangle midline, target equal to the triangle's opening height (measured move). Differs from the dead Bollinger squeeze: detection is geometric convergence of extremes, entry requires the boundary break, and the target is structural.
CHART-5 Conviction bar follow-through (marubozu / passant de ceinture): a bar whose body fills at least 85 percent of its range with the range itself at least 1.5 ATR is an initiative bar that consumed the book's liquidity on one side; enter in its direction at the close, stop at the bar's midpoint (an initiative bar that gets retraced halfway is failed), trail at 2 ATR, time-exit after 10 bars. Tests pure conviction-bar drift independent of any surrounding pattern.
