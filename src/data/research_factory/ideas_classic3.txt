CLASSIC-1 Fibonacci retracement pullback: identify the most recent significant swing (rolling N-bar high and low); in an uptrend, when price retraces into the 38.2-61.8 percent zone of the swing and prints a reversal bar (close back above the prior bar high), buy with a stop just beyond the next deeper Fibonacci level (61.8 if entered at 50, or 78.6 if entered at 61.8) and take profit at the prior swing extreme; mirror for downtrends. Edge claim: retracement zones concentrate resting limit orders from traders anchoring on the same widely-watched ratios, creating self-fulfilling support; the reversal-bar trigger avoids catching falling knives inside the zone.
CLASSIC-2 Impulse continuation with pullback pyramid (mechanical core of Elliott impulse waves; true wave counting is subjective and is NOT attempted): after a strong directional thrust (close beyond the prior M-bar extreme with above-median range, i.e. wave-1 proxy), buy the first pullback that holds above 61.8 percent retracement of the thrust (wave-2 proxy), add once when price breaks the thrust high (wave-3 proxy), trail the combined position at the 38.2 percent retracement of the extended move, and exit fully on a momentum divergence or trail hit (wave-5 proxy). Edge claim: initial thrusts that survive a shallow retracement attract continuation flow; pyramiding converts the strongest leg into convexity. Requires pyramiding: costs_to_model exclusive_orders false.
CLASSIC-3 Triple top and triple bottom reversal (three mountains / three rivers): detect three local peaks of similar height (within a tolerance band) separated by intervening troughs over a lookback window; enter short only when price confirms by closing below the lowest intervening trough (the neckline), with a stop just above the highest peak and a target measured one pattern-height below the neckline; mirror with three similar bottoms for longs. Edge claim: repeated failure at the same level exhausts the buying side and stops cluster beyond the neckline; the confirmation close avoids shorting into an intact range.
