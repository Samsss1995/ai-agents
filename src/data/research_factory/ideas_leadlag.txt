# Evidence batch idea 4. Datasets: ETH-XL-1h SOL-XL-1h (BtcClose column).
EVID-4 BTC-to-alt lead-lag: information enters the most liquid asset first; the BtcClose column gives BTC's concurrent price on the alt's own bars. When BTC has moved more than 1.5 ATR-equivalents over the last 6 hours while this alt's own move over the same window is less than half of BTC's in proportional terms, the alt is a documented laggard; enter the alt in BTC's direction, exit on proportional convergence or after 12 bars, ATR stop. The signal is RELATIVE and conditional - not a directional market bet.
