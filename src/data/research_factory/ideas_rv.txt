# Evidence batch idea 1. Datasets: ETHBTC-1h SOLBTC-1h (ratio bars, two-leg costs).
EVID-1 Crypto relative-value spread reversion: the ETH/BTC and SOL/BTC price ratios are market-neutral series - directional crypto regime risk, the failure mode that killed every prior crypto strategy, cancels between the legs; deviations of the ratio beyond two standard deviations of its trailing 30-day mean reflect temporary flow imbalances that revert over days. Enter toward the mean only at extreme z-scores with a half-z stop and exit at the mean, multi-day holds, few trades. IMPORTANT: each trade has TWO legs, so set costs_to_model commission 0.0024 (double the standard) - never lower.
